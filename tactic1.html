FC Cincinnati's Tactics:

April 26, 2024

FC Cincinnati currently play's a 5-2-1-2. The 5 represents the 3 centerbacks and 2 wingbacks, the 2 represents the holding midfielders, the 1 represents the attacking midfielder, and the second 2 represents the two strikers.
