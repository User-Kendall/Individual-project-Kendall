About Me:

My name is Joel Kendall. I am 20 years old. I am currently a student at Northern Kentucky University and a member of the NKU soccer club. I have played soccer for the last 17 years and have been watching professional matches for the last 10.

I am also a season ticket holder for FC Cincinnati. I have watched them since their founding in 2015. Over that period of time, I have learned a lot about the club and the league. This website will be used to discuss my opinions and that tactics of FC Cincinnati, the MLS, and soccer in general.
