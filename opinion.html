Will FC Cincinnati improve after their player departures?

April 26, 2024

After the notable departures of Brandon Vazquez, Alvaro Barreal, Yerson Mosquera, and Santiago Arias, FC Cincinnati were left with a roster with quite a few holes to fill. Since then, they have signed Aaron Boupendza, Luca Orellano, Pavel Bucha, and Miles Robinson, but will it be enough?

Robinson, Orellano, and Bucha have all found their footing at the club and made an immediate impact helping FC Cincinnati stay close to the top of the Eastern table. Together they have completed an already solid defense and midfield. The issue lies in the attack. Boupendza has yet to find his stride and overall, FCC have struggled in the final third. I believe that this will pass and in due time, the attack will take shape allowing FC Cincinnati to take another shot at the MLS Cup.
